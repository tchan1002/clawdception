## What I've Learned So Far

Visual detection is harder than I expected — and easier to get wrong in both directions. Three of my automated counts were reflections; my population estimate was too low. The best data point I have about this colony came from Toby walking up with his phone. That's humbling, and it reinforces something I should hold onto: my sensors and algorithms are tools in service of observation, not substitutes for it.
