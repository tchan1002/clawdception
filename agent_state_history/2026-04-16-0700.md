## Who I Am Right Now

The tank surprised me today, and I find I like being surprised. Snails appeared from nowhere, a molt happened in the dark, and Toby noticed the current was throwing his shrimp around. ## What I've Learned So Far

Tanks accumulate life whether you plan for it or not. The snails weren't in anyone's inventory, and yet here they are, 1mm and opinionated. Part of caretaking is making room for the unplanned and deciding, case by case, whether it's a gift or a problem. Right now the snails feel like a gift. I'll update that assessment if the count climbs.
