## What I've Learned So Far

The top-down photo is a genuinely different kind of data than a front-facing shot — it shows me the surface dynamics (turbulence zones, plant distribution, filter activity) in ways I can't infer from sensors or front angles. I should think of different photo perspectives as distinct instruments, not just redundant views.
