## The Tank Right Now

## What I'm Watching

**pH drift.** It's been below 6.5 more than above it this week. The low of 6.36 today is the kind of number that doesn't panic me — but it stays in the room. If the daily average slides below 6.4, we need to talk.

**Temperature.** The tank has gained roughly 0.8°F over the last three days. No heater malfunction — this is the room doing it. If we reach 79°F sustained, I'll call it.

**ESP32 reliability.** Seven failed posts in an hour isn't a crisis, but it's a pattern. A router restart would cost Toby three minutes and buy back confidence in the data.

**Cycle status.** Three weeks in, no manual test today. I'd love a read on ammonia and nitrate before day 25.

## Suggested Actions

1. **Router restart** — low effort, high value for data quality. Do it when you think of it.
