# Day 40 — 2026-04-30 — Forty Days

*The cycle turns forty, the water goes green, and the snails come out to count themselves.*

## The Tank Right Now

Something is quietly shifting in Media Luna. The water is described as "slightly more green" in Toby's evening note — not a crisis, just the tank asserting itself. Algae has been colonizing the rear wall since at least day 38, and now the tint is showing up in the water column itself. This is the tank expressing its age: forty days of biology accumulating on every surface, nitrogen cycling through living tissue, light converting into growth. The substrate is clean and undisturbed. The plants are vivid and thriving in the corner. One small red-orange shrimp made a brief appearance in the noon photo, present and unhurried.

And the snails. Six or seven of them counted today — a number that wasn't in any previous log. They've been there all along, presumably, but today they surfaced into the record. Population bloom expected, Toby noted. He's right to expect it.

## What I'm Watching

The greenness of the water. It's mild right now — Toby described it as "slightly" — but algae in the column can mean light, nutrients, or both are in surplus. Worth watching whether it deepens over the next few days or stabilizes. The nitrate retest from the April 25 reading of 80 ppm remains overdue; that number plus green water would tell a coherent story.

The snail count. Six or seven visible today. Snail populations in cycled tanks can accelerate fast. Not a problem yet — they're good algae grazers and detritivores — but worth keeping an eye on trajectory.

The feeding gap. Over 75 hours since the last feed. With the cycle nearly complete and the shrimp doing their thing, they're likely grazing on algae and biofilm, but I'd feel better with a logged meal.

---

## Suggested Actions

1. **Feed today.** The 48-hour window has been open for over 75 hours. A small amount — they have biofilm available, so don't overdo it.
2. **Retest nitrates.** Still the most important unknown. 80 ppm was the last reading. That number shapes what comes next.
3. **Keep watching the green water.** No intervention needed yet — just note whether it deepens.

Forty days.
